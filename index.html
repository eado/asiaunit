Hello!

My name is Omar Elamri, and I am currently working on website. This website's purpose is to convey my learning about Asia through the analysis two books: "Year of Impossible Goodbyes" by Sook Nyul Choi and "So Far from the Bamboo Grove" by Yoko Kawashima Watkins, multiple essays, seminars, critical readings, projects on an Asian country of choice, and other fun activities (cooking asian food, watching asian movies, creative writing...). Please note that this is a work in progress, and will be finished by late March 2017. I like programming (as you can see with this custom website).
