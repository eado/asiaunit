Blog Post 1: Chapter 3

2/14/2017

I hate sickness. Sickness cripples, divides, and makes you powerless. Speaking of being powerless, I thought earlier: "Captain Narita and his men had such power over our lives" (Choi 32). I haven't heard from Grandfather for a few hours. I hope he's doing okay. While I am waiting in the yard, I wonder: Is it even worth living as a Korean? I mean, look at these Japanese, they are oppressing us to the limit where we don't have a point. Grandfather always had hope of the war being over, effectively ending Japanese occupation; but I feel that hope is very improbable. I feel bad thinking of these terrible thoughts, but the Japanese are really pushing it! Well, right now, I am being called up to Grandfather's room. Finally! Maybe I'll get some answers....
